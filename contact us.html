A Brother Kebab & Pizza offers a delightful array of Kebab cuisine that you can savor every day. Located at 5A Chester Hill Rd, Chester Hill NSW, we provide delivery and pickup services throughout the week.

Our menu features a variety of pizzas including Chester Hill Special, Australian Pizza, Meat Lovers, Mexicana, Beef Kebab, Margarita, and more. Don't miss out on our gourmet King Prawn pizza!

Craving something different? Try our Chicken Pide, Mushroom Pide, Lamb Shish Kebab, Chicken Shish Kebab, and other mouthwatering options.
